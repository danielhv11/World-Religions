15 Jewish Facts

1- Judaism has several different sects

2- Shabbat is the Jewish holy day

3- The Torah is the holy book of Judaism

4- Judaism is one of the three Abrahamic religions

5- The Temple Mount is the holiest site for Jews

6- The Jewish people began as slaves according to the book of Exodus

7- The Bar Mitzvah is the rite of passage into adulthood

8- There are 613 Commandments in the Law of Moses

9- Synagogues are the place of worship in Judaism

10- There is no original Torah existing today

11- The spiritual leader of Judaism is known as Rabbi

12- Hanukkah is a Jewish holiday known as the festival of lights

13- The religious symbol of Judaism is the Star of David

14- There are three pilgrimage festivals in Judaism

15- The Western Wall is a holy site for prayer and pilgrimage
